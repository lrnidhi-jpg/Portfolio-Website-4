Design System

The main aim was to improve accessibility by using high-contrast color choices and larger tap targets. Consistent UI patterns were applied to reduce cognitive load

Key Feature- 1

Smoother

Onboarding

The onboarding flow was redesigned to reduce friction for first-time users, improving clarity and completion rates.

Key Feature- 2

Organized Communities

The community pages were designed to showcase members’ interests, helping them connect more easily and engage in richer interactions with others.

Key Feature-3

Connect with anyone, anywhere!

The design was made responsive to ensure that the website could be viewed seamlessly on any device

Key Feature-4

Rewards

A rewards system was introduced to increase community engagement and incentivize meaningful contributions. ‘Nblik coins’ could be earned by users for writing posts and engaging with the community.
