Better Plates

Better Plates’ was the outcome of the ‘Eats and Algorithms’ studio project by RCA and Sodexo. The project aimed to design solutions for digitising the food consumer experience to create a sustainable future. The outcomes were evaluated based on their feasibility and potential for integration into Sodexo’s operations.

What if we could reshape food culture at work to reflect the values people hold, through daily small choices?

Research

Coming from diverse design backgrounds, Leerang, Dhika, and I kicked things off with some simple desk research. It helped us get a handle on the issues around food waste in offices and also worked as a great icebreaker for the team!

User Study

12 office workers were surveyed and their responses were analysed

55%

felt unsure whether cafeteria meals were “sustainable” or “healthy.”

User Journey Mapping

We mapped office workers’ user journeys across key moments, such as planning lunch, ordering at work, and checking their fridge at home.

Lack of healthy and sustainability info

No personalization

No visible progress

No community or shared experience

Opportunities

The results of our research were in! This revealed common pain points faced by office workers regarding their food habits

Service Flow

Better Plates blends personalization, instant feedback, and playful nudges- all within the trusted Sodexo Everyday App.

Storyboard

Better Plates makes healthy eating easy with personalized tips, progress tracking, and community rewards- all built into daily life

Ideation

We explored multiple sketches and low-fidelity wireframes to narrow down the style and aesthetic that best suited the project

Key Feature 1 -

Eating Habit Test

We created a simple and engaging Eating Habits Food Test, inspired by the MBTI personality framework. The test captured users’ dietary preferences in a fun, interactive way and assigned them unique food avatars.

Key Feature 2 -

Food Avatars

We created food avatars -playful little characters based on your eating habits, that made personalized meal recommendations feel fun and easy

"I'm a 'Health Hacker'. I need enough energy to hit the gym after work!"

"I'm a 'Convenient Sustainer'. I just need something quick before my next meeting!

Key Feature 3 -

Leftover Recipe

Got leftovers from lunch? The app turns them into brand-new recipe ideas so nothing goes to waste. Also, you get a surprise dinner!

Key Feature 4 -

Leaderboard

A weekly leaderboard shows who in the office had the lowest food carbon footprint, turning sustainable eating into a friendly competition

Impact

At the end of the day, we wanted our project to create a positive impact on both people and the environment

For a company like Sodexo, integrating Better Plates could improve employee satisfaction while aligning with corporate sustainability goals

By encouraging recipe reuse and sustainable menu choices, Better Plates could help office workers cut down on food waste, reducing both landfill contribution and carbon emissions.
