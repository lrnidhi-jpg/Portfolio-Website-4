COCOPOD Crib

COCOPOD is a special crib made out of reclaimed wood that can be converted into a cabinet once the child grows up. It addresses the issue of furniture waste while providing a solution that is in line with circular economy, thus promoting sustainable furniture

This was a group project done in collaboration with Rishita Sharma, Soumya Garg and George Paul

The Problem

Across the world, 4-5% of household waste in landfills is furniture. We can estimate that in India, we generate up to 3.1 million tonnes of furniture waste per year

Brief

To design sustainable baby furniture which accommodates the baby's growth and can be put to other use by the members of the family.

What does a Crib mean?

Cribs in India have a lot of sentimental value and people prefer to keep them even after the infant grows up. My grandmother still has my crib!

Concept Development

Being in charge of 3D modelling and form development, I built a 1:10 scale model of the crib design and made the necessary adjustments to the final model
