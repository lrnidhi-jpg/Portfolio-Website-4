Kagaz Paper Recycling Machine

Kagaz is a home an office friendly paper recycling machine that aims to make paper recycling more accessible in areas where a lot of paper waste is generated. Paper is a resource used world wide. However, many areas lack recycling plants and do not have adequate collection facilities to gather waste paper and ensure that it gets recycled. Kagaz tries to eliminate the middle man and allow the user to recycle paper at their own will.

Finding Technologies

In order to build a paper recycling machine, I decided to find devices that could be used in the process. I finalized on a Paper shredder, a blender and a sandwich press

Concept Development

The next step was to find a way to make the machine work. I tried to imagine how paper recycling could be contained to a single machine

Finding a Form

The challenge was to find a form that not only looked like it would fit well in an office environment, but also accommodate the internal mechanism

Final Renders

In order to fit into an office environment, I experimented with different colours and textures. I finally settled on a neutral colour scheme that would suit most offices
