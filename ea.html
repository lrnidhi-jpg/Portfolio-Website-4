Why did I choose this?

Seeing a flawed device in the bio medical field, where thousands of people can be gravely impacted by a single error was truly alarming. The display and controls could sometimes result in errors. That is why I decided to redesign the device and make it more user- friendly.

Brief

About the Electrolyte Analyser

Electrolyte analyzers are used for the measurement of electrolyte levels in the human body to detect metabolic imbalances and measure renal and cardiac functions.

To redesign the display and controls of an electrolyte analyzer to make the process of using the machine more legible, intuitive, easy to change and navigate

Research

I visited 3 different pathology labs and interviewed the technicians to understand how the machine works
