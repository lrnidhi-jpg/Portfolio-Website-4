Special Projects

Special Projects

Better Plates

Sodexo and Royal College of Art

November 2024- May 2025

London, UK

Better Plates is the outcome of a collaborative project with Sodexo, where we developed solutions to encourage sustainable eating habits among office employees.


It featured a simple Eating Habits Test and playful food avatars to gamify the experience of making carbon-friendly meal choices while supporting employee health.


This project was created in collaboration with Leerang Lim and Jasmine Bunga Mahardhika.

Pitching our Project to Sodexo (Picture Credit: Royal College of Art)

Think Climate Chair

Royal College of Art- Across RCA

October 2024- March 2025

London, UK

In response to the alarming rise in global temperatures, we created a collaborative piece titled Think Climate. The chair uses thermochromic fabrics and pigments that shift from black to transparent, revealing the users imprint once they sit, serving as a metaphor for the lasting impact humans have on the environment.


This project was a collaboration with Mark Woollam, Qiongnan Wu, and Po Wang.

Think Climate chair (Picture credit: Po Wang)

Litter Lodges

Royal College of Art- Grand Challenge

January 2025

London, UK

We collaborated with RSPB Bexley founders, Mr. Ralph Todd and Mrs. Brenda Todd, to study the diverse ecosystem of the London Borough of Bexley. Our proposal introduced street furniture made from recycled litter, integrated with habitats for birds and small animals, providing local fauna with new spaces to live and thrive.


This project was a collaboration with Yi-Hsuan Wu, Grace McAllister, Hyein Lee, and Jiayuan Qiu.



Studying the Marshes of Erith (Picture credit: Yi-Hsuan Wu)

E-Waste Volunteer

ENSYDE and SAAHAS

July 2017

Bangalore, India
